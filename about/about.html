About us

Real-Time Asset Tracking. Community-Governed. Built for the Algorand Ecosystem.

Our Mission

To deliver real-time valuations and complete asset visibility for every ASA and NFT on Algorand — empowering users, developers, and investors to manage portfolios with confidence, transparency, and full data sovereignty.

What We Offer

✅ Real-time ASA & NFT valuations from all major Algorand DEXs
✅ Comprehensive portfolio tracking across wallets, DeFi protocols, and smart contracts
✅ Bundle management — group and label multiple addresses with custom names
✅ Mobile apps for iOS and Android
✅ Developer API (v2) — fetch valuations, bundle data, NFT pricing, and more
✅ DAO-backed governance — limited-access, decentralized structure with no central owner


DAO Governance (2025)

The ASA Stats DAO is live, with core infrastructure and treasury now operating under a limited-governor model.

Current State

• DAO governance was established through long-term staking of ≥500,000 ASASTATS over 10 months.
• The initial governor cohort is fixed; no new seats are currently being added.
• DAO privileges are active via on-chain smart contracts (Permission, Subtopia).
• On-chain proposal voting is in development and planned for release in a future update.

Why Access Is Limited

“The DAO is intended to remain stable. New seats will be added only under specific proposals, voted on by existing governors. Membership is intentionally exclusive, transparent, and locked unless expanded through formal on-chain governance.” — ASA Stats Medium: New DAO Seat Model

DAO Governance discussions are all publicly available on GitHub.

Infrastructure & Design Principles

• Near real-time block sync
• Direct-chain data reads (not reliant on third-party APIs)
• Planned custom Kubernetes scaling for high uptime and user traffic surges

Everything shown in the platform’s UI is powered by the same API available to developers and advanced users.

Team & Funding

“ASA Stats no longer belongs to one person — it belongs to its governors and users.”

Community-Driven, Feature-Led

• Most features originate from public GitHub discussions
• ASASTATS token rewards are granted for valid feature requests, bug reports, and contributions

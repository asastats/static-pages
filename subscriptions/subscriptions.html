Subscription Tiers

All subscription payments are made in ASASTATS (token ID: 393537671) and processed via Subtopia smart contracts. Subscriptions grant automated, on-chain permission access for features.


How Subscriptions Work (Under the Hood)

ASA Stats uses:

• Subtopia smart contracts (per tier) for subscription validation
• The Permission dApp to map wallets to access levels
• Automatic refresh of your tier based on your wallet state

All verification is on-chain and permissionless.

How to Subscribe

1. Hold enough ASASTATS in your wallet.
2. Select your tier from the Subscription Model below and confirm payment via WalletConnect
3. Your permission level updates automatically

You can view or manage your tier at any time through the dashboard.
